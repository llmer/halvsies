Halv/sies

Privacy policy · Effective June 11, 2026

The short version: Halvsies collects nothing about you. There is no long version, but the details follow.

Data collection

Halvsies does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. It makes no network requests of its own.

What stays on your device

Your game progress — level bests, streaks, blade timers, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple’s UserDefaults system, as declared in the app’s privacy manifest. It never leaves your device. Deleting the app deletes it.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details. Purchases can be restored on any device signed into the same App Store account.

Children

Because Halvsies collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be posted at this address with an updated effective date.
