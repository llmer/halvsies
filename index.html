Halv/sies

Support

Halvsies is a game about one clean cut: drag a straight line through a shape and get scored on how close your split lands to 50/50. Walk the Path of seeded levels, or take on the Daily — the same shape for every player in the world, once a day.

Common questions

How do blades work? You hold up to five. A Path attempt borrows one blade; pass the level and it comes back, fail and it breaks. Broken blades return on their own, one every twenty minutes.

Is the Daily ever locked? No. The Daily never costs a blade and is never paywalled.

I bought the Master Blade on another device. Open the refill sheet and tap “restore purchases.” Purchases follow your App Store account.

Will I lose progress if I delete the app? Yes — progress lives only on your device. Purchases are always restorable from the App Store.

Contact

Write to support@llmer.com. Bug reports, scores you’re proud of, and shapes you’d like to see are all welcome.
